The Retreat

Authentic Croatian Stone Architecture & Contemporary Comfort

Located in the breathtaking coastal town of Baška on Krk Island, Villa Posija represents a masterclass in regional heritage design. Crafted using local hand-carved stone, the villa integrates seamlessly with the surrounding silver olive groves, wild rosemary bushes, and rocky Mediterranean hills, offering absolute seclusion and a connection to the Adriatic coast.

Authentic Stone Masonry

Traditional thick stone wall architecture ensuring natural insulation and rustic coastal elegance.

Heated Infinity Pool

Hovering over the coastline with integrated warm water climate regulation for year-round swims.

Unrivaled Sea Views

Stunning vistas of the clear blue waters of the Adriatic and the nearby islands.

Mediterranean Flora

Landscaped private estate featuring aromatic gardens with lavender, figs, and olive trees.
